Gameplay

What can you do in GMod Tower?
  • Play Gamemodes
    • PVP
    • Ballrace
    • Virus
    • UCH (Ultimate Chimera Hunt)
    • Zombie Massacre
    • And many more to come!
  • Watch Youtube (Either in the Theater, or in the Suites)
  • Listen to the Radio
  • Play Trivia, Tetris, Tic-Tac-Toe and a range of Arcade Games
  • Buy items for your suite
  • Buy player models, jetpacks, body manipulating potions, and other personal items!
  • Gamble at the Casino
  • Duel other players for money or for fun
  • Get a drink from the bar (and get drunk)
  • Earn achievements and milestones
  • Play different instruments such as a piano
  • Play lobby minigames
    • Snowball Battle
    • Chainsaw Battle
    • Plane Battle
    • Obama Smash
    • Balloon Pop
Is there mic support?
Yes there is. Voice chat is enabled everywhere.
How can I play in 3rd person?
You can use your scroll wheel to move in between third person and first person at a range of different distances.

Alternatively, you can bind a key to the 'gmt_thirdperson' command in console. (To enable console, Go to Options > Keyboard tab > Advanced > Enable Developer Console. Then press ` on your keyboard to open the console.)

e.g. bind z 'gmt_thirdperson'
Is there a way I can see my hat and other items on myself?
Yes, there is a way. Hold right click while in lobby to enter view self mode. Then you can just rotate your mouse to view yourself at all angles.
How do I earn GMC in-game?
There's different ways to earn GMC:
  • Gamemodes
  • Minigames
  • Tetris
  • Casino
  • Trivia
  • Achievements
  • Special events

Common Issues

Why am I lagging on the server?
The central server for GMod Tower hosts a multitude of events. These events can cause spikes in network usage on the server, which we are working on fixing. Due to the complexity and the high amount of players connected - you may experience network lag.

Most of the time FPS related issues will be low, but can occur. GMod Tower has lots of things going on that will affect your client. Simply put, however, if you can run CS:S at a decent rate, you should be okay for the majority of GMod Tower.

If you are watching a video without a decent internet connection, you will lag. Streaming videos come from YouTube and other services.
While joining tower I get 'missing maps map/gmt_build---.bsp, disconnecting'
If you see this error, you're missing the client content pack. You can download it here.
How do I adjust the volume of the TV or Theater?
Simply hold "Q" and adjust the volume slider. This slider will appear if you are in the Theater or if you are looking at the Suite TV.
I and many others hate the video currently playing in the theater - can we vote to change it?
You can change the video only if the theater is running in YouTube mode.

Just press "Q" while in the theater and click the Vote Remove button. The vote remove system is based on a 2/3 rule.

However during such events such as Movie Nights and Cartoon Cartoon Fridays, the vote remove system is overridden and only Admins can remove a video.
I crashed in a gamemode, can I return?
Yes, you can. If the game you were playing is still going, you can simply rejoin the gamemode's server.
I'm seeing errors or missing textures.
Go to the Downloads page on our website and hit "subscribe to all" on our GMT workshop collection.

If you're still having issues, make sure you have Counter Strike: Source and Team Fortress 2 installed and mounted beforing joining. They are both required to play GMod Tower without errors.
The TV, theater, radio and arcades are black, what's wrong?
You need to change the gmod branch to chromium version.

Project

Do I need to pay to play GMod Tower? What about donating?
No. GMod Tower is free. And we don't accept donations, The Community First is a fanmade rehost of original gmod tower so we can't morally and legally accept donation.
Is this all on one map/server?
Not entirely. There's one central server hosting all the suites and stores. The other servers each host a single gamemode (or multiple gamemodes) depending on gamemode requirements.
How does this all work?
You simply join our own gamemodes from the central server. All of your player data is automatically saved and loaded from a central database based on your SteamID, so your stats, awards and inventory will be instantly available on any GMod Tower server.
