Before you connect to GMT...
You're going to need to meet the requirements below.
  • Garry's Mod with Counter Strike: Source and Team Fortress 2 mounted
  • 2 GB RAM
  • At least 5GB of hard drive space with at least 1GB of additional space for future updates
  • Steady Internet connection with a decent download speed for streaming media
You also need to download the content. There's a few options for you available below.
GMT Workshop Collection


Recommended download for all users.


Instructions:

  • Login to Steam
  • Click "Subscribe to All"

Benefits of Workshop:

  • It's quick and easy!
  • Automatically keeps you up to date.
  • It's tied to your steam account and will redownload if you reinstall GMod.
  • Everything is compressed when downloading.
  • You can use certain features of tower in Sandbox.
  • Requires no external program - you just need GMod.
  • Compatible with OSX.
